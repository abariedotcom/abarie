The Story

COMEDY
WAS ALWAYS
IN HIM

Born in Lagos and raised with deep Nnewi, Anambra State roots, Ifeanyichukwu Daniel Okeke — the man the world knows as Abarie.com — didn't discover comedy. Comedy found him. On construction sites, in market stalls, and through years of humble hustle, his gift for making people laugh was simply undeniable.

His career ignited in 2021 and has not slowed since. Today he stands as one of Nigeria's sharpest stand-up voices and most in-demand comperes — a headline act who has shared stages with Nigeria's biggest comedy names and who owns his show franchise, So Far So Good.

"From selling vegetables to fend for my large family, to being a labourer at construction sites — I never let my reality then judge my future."

— Abarie.com, Saturday Beats / Punch Nigeria
